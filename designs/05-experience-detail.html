About the Experience

Prepare for a truly unique and exhilarating experience: the fastest zip line in the world since 2013! Soar over Penrhyn Quarry, where you could travel at speeds of over 100 mph while taking in the breathtaking views of Snowdonia.

Velocity 2 is the first of its kind, featuring four parallel lines that span 1.5 kilometers over the bright blue quarry lake. You'll feel the ultimate rush of flying as you launch from the top and descend towards the bottom, head-first!
