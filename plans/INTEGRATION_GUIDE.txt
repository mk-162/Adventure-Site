QUICK CONTENT POPULATION STRATEGY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

For each destination page, include:

1. ACCOMMODATION SECTION
   - Booking.com search widget for that location
   - 3-5 featured properties with affiliate deep links
   - Price range indicator

2. ACTIVITIES SECTION
   - GetYourGuide widget for local activities
   - 3-5 featured experiences
   - Price and duration info

3. REVIEWS SECTION
   - TripAdvisor review widget
   - Social proof from real travelers

4. GEAR GUIDE SECTION
   - Amazon product recommendations
   - "What to bring" list
   - Affiliate product links

5. WEATHER SECTION
   - Met Office weather widget
   - Seasonal activity recommendations

This gives you a complete, revenue-generating page with minimal original content!
