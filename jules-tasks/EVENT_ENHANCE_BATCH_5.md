## For Each Event, Capture:

### 1. Coordinates (REQUIRED)
- Exact venue location
- Christmas markets: town center coordinates

### 2. Hero Image (REQUIRED)
- Festive imagery from official sources
- **NO AI-generated images**
- Christmas lights, markets, winter scenes

### 3. Social Links
- Facebook events (Christmas markets often have dedicated FB events)
- Instagram

### 4. Category
Use: `family` or `festival`

### 5. 2025-26 Season Dates
- Most run Nov-Dec or Dec-Jan
- Note typical date range

### 6. Ticket/Pricing Info
- Free entry vs ticketed
- Typical prices for attractions (light trails, Santa trains)

Save each to `data/events/enhanced/{slug}.json`
