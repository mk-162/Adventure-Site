The fastest zip line in the world

Prepare for a truly unique and exhilarating experience. Velocity 2 is the fastest zip line in the world and the longest in Europe. You'll fly over Penrhyn Quarry at speeds of over 100mph while taking in breathtaking views of Snowdonia.

Velocity 2 is the first of its kind, featuring four parallel lines that span 1.5 kilometers over the bright blue quarry lake. You'll feel the ultimate rush of flying as you launch from the top and descend towards the bottom, head-first!
