# Family Adventure Week

This Snowdonia adventure is designed for families who want to experience the best of North Wales without pushing their limits *too* much. Think stunning scenery, exhilarating activities, and plenty of opportunities for memory-making. Get ready for zip lines, underground trampolines, gentle hikes, and maybe even a splash in a mountain stream!

## Day 1: Arrival & Underground Fun

### Morning
**Activity:** Bounce Below
Descend into the depths of a slate mine and bounce your way through giant trampolines! It's a unique and unforgettable experience that's fun for all ages. Book your tickets in advance to avoid disappointment.

**Travel:** 10 min drive to Blaenau Ffestiniog

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Underground Adventure
Stay underground! Explore the Llechwedd Slate Caverns on foot, learning about the lives of the miners who worked here. A fascinating and slightly spooky insight into Welsh history.

### Evening
**Stay:** Bunkhouse
Settle into your bunkhouse accommodation, a great option for families on a budget. It's a chance to relax and bond after a day of adventure.

**Dinner suggestion:** The Pengwern Arms - classic pub fare with mountain views.

---

## Day 2: Zipping Through the Forest

### Morning
**Activity:** Zip World Fforest Coaster
Take a thrilling toboggan ride through the forest! This gravity-driven coaster is a fantastic way to experience the woodland scenery. Kids and adults will both love it.

**Travel:** 20 min drive to Zip World Fforest

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Gorge Walking
Cool off and challenge yourselves with a gorge walking adventure. Scramble up waterfalls, slide down rocks, and swim in crystal-clear pools. Expect to get wet!

### Evening
**Stay:** Bunkhouse
Relax, dry off and get ready for tomorrow.

**Dinner suggestion:** Yr Hen Felin - pizzas and tasty pub snacks in a cozy setting.

---

## Day 3: Lakeside Stroll & Mountain Biking

### Morning
**Activity:** Llanberis Path Guided Walk
Enjoy a gentle hike along the Llanberis Path, a popular route up Snowdon. This is a great option for families who want to experience the mountain scenery without a strenuous climb.

**Travel:** 30 min drive to Llanberis

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Mountain Biking - Temtiwr
Head to the Gwydir Forest and try the Temtiwr mountain bike trail. It's a short, fun trail perfect for beginners and families. Enjoy the beautiful forest scenery as you pedal along.

### Evening
**Stay:** Campsite/Bunkhouse
Pitch your tent or settle into your bunkhouse, enjoying the peace and quiet of the countryside.

**Dinner suggestion:** Cook your own BBQ at the campsite or bunkhouse.

---

## Day 4: Adrenaline Rush!

### Morning
**Activity:** Velocity 2 Zip Line
Experience the thrill of flying through the air on Velocity 2, the fastest zip line in the world! This is a truly unforgettable experience. Minimum height and weight restrictions apply - check before booking.

**Travel:** 30 min drive to Penrhyn Quarry

### Afternoon
**Activity:** White Water Rafting
Get your adrenaline pumping with a white water rafting adventure on the River Tryweryn. This is a fun and exciting activity that's suitable for families with older children.

### Evening
**Stay:** Activity Centre
Enjoy the facilities at your activity centre accommodation.

**Dinner suggestion:** The activity centre restaurant - convenient and often offers family-friendly options.

---

## Day 5: Farewell Snowdonia

### Morning
**Activity:** Free time in Betws-y-Coed
Explore the charming village of Betws-y-Coed, known as the "Gateway to Snowdonia." Browse the shops, visit the Swallow Falls, or simply relax by the river.

**Travel:** Depart from Betws-y-Coed.

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Safe travels home!

### Evening
**Stay:** N/A

**Dinner suggestion:** N/A

## Tips

- Book activities and accommodation well in advance, especially during peak season.
- Pack for all types of weather – Snowdonia can be unpredictable! Layers are key.
- Check out local farmers' markets for delicious Welsh produce and treats.
