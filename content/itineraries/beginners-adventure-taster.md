# Beginner's Adventure Taster

Ready to experience the magic of Snowdonia without pushing yourself to the limit? This 2-day itinerary is perfect for adventure newbies. We're talking stunning scenery, a taste of adrenaline, and memories that'll last a lifetime. Get ready to explore underground, whizz through the trees, and enjoy the best of North Wales!

## Day 1: Underground Wonders & Forest Fun

### Morning
**Activity:** Bounce Below
Dive into a world of bouncy nets in a disused slate mine! Bounce Below is a unique and fun experience, perfect for all ages and fitness levels. Expect a workout and plenty of laughs as you explore this subterranean playground.

**Travel:** 5 min drive to Zip World Fforest

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Zip World Fforest Coaster
After bouncing underground, it's time for some above-ground thrills. The Fforest Coaster is a gravity-driven toboggan run through the woods. It's fast, fun, and suitable for all ages – a perfect way to end the afternoon!

### Evening
**Stay:** Campsite/Bunkhouse
Pitch your tent or grab a bunk at a local campsite/bunkhouse. It's a great way to connect with nature and unwind after a day of adventure.

**Dinner suggestion:** The Stables Bar & Restaurant - Pub grub with a Welsh twist.

---

## Day 2: Gentle Giants & Scenic Views

### Morning
**Activity:** Mountain Biking - Temtiwr
Enjoy a relaxed morning cycle on the Temtiwr trail, a great introduction to mountain biking. The trail is short and sweet, perfect for beginners. Remember to check the bike over before setting off!

**Travel:** 30 min drive to Llanberis

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Llanberis Path Guided Walk
Take in the stunning views from Snowdon with a guided walk up the Llanberis Path. This is the easiest route to the summit, and the guide will provide fascinating insights into the area's history and geology. No need to rush just enjoy the views.

### Evening
**Stay:** Hostel
Relax after a day on the mountain at a local hostel. Share adventure stories with fellow travellers and unwind after an awesome adventure.

**Dinner suggestion:** Pete's Eats - A classic mountaineering cafe serving hearty, unfussy meals.
