Best Time to Visit Snowdonia: A Complete Guide

Everything you need to know about weather, crowds, and seasonal activities in Wales' premier national park.

Gareth Jones Updated April 15, 2024 • 8 min read
Mount Snowdon Summit

Snowdonia National Park (Eryri) is a year-round destination, but the best time for your visit depends heavily on what you want to do. Whether you are chasing the summit of Snowdon, looking for waterfalls, or seeking cozy pub fires, the experience changes drastically with the seasons.

While summer offers the most reliable weather, it also brings the biggest crowds. Conversely, winter provides solitude but demands serious respect for the mountain conditions. Here is our detailed breakdown to help you plan your perfect Welsh adventure.

filter_drama Spring (March - May)

Spring is often cited by locals as the secret season. The waterfalls are in full force due to spring rains, and the landscape transforms into a vibrant green. It's an excellent time for photography and lower-level walks.

info Pro Tip

May is statistically one of the driest months in Wales, making it a sweet spot before the summer school holidays begin.

sunny Summer (June - August)

Summer brings the longest days, with daylight lasting until 10 PM. This is peak season for hiking Snowdon, meaning parking lots fill up by 7 AM. However, the Snowdon Sherpa bus service runs most frequently during this time.

nature_people Autumn (September - November)

The heather turns purple and the bracken turns gold. September often keeps some summer warmth, but by November, conditions can become wintry on the high peaks. This is the best season for serious photographers.

ac_unit Winter (December - February)

Winter in Snowdonia is strictly for the prepared. The peaks are often snow-capped, requiring crampons and ice axes. However, the valleys offer cozy pubs with roaring fires and hearty Welsh cawl (stew).

The Final Verdict

If you can avoid school holidays, late May and September offer the perfect balance of reasonable weather and manageable crowds. For the absolute best weather, aim for June, but book your accommodation months in advance.
