# Summit Challenge

This trip is designed for experienced adventurers seeking a serious challenge in the heart of Snowdonia. Expect demanding hikes, exposed scrambles, and breathtaking views from the highest peaks. We'll push our limits physically and mentally, rewarding ourselves with incredible scenery and a sense of accomplishment. Get ready to earn those summit selfies!

## Day 1: Ascending to Heights

### Morning
**Activity:** Snowdon Summit Hike
We're kicking off our adventure with a full-on ascent of Snowdon, the highest peak in Wales. We'll choose a challenging route (Watkin Path perhaps?) and pace ourselves for a strenuous but rewarding climb. Prepare for diverse terrain and changing weather conditions.

**Travel:** 15 min drive to Watkin Path Trailhead

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Relax & Refuel
After conquering Snowdon, we'll head down to a local pub for a well-deserved hearty meal and some local ales. We'll need to recharge those batteries for the next couple of days!

### Evening
**Stay:** Bunkhouse
We'll be staying in a rugged bunkhouse near Llanberis for the next two nights. Simple, comfortable, and perfect for a group of adventurers.

**Dinner suggestion:** Pete's Eats - A legendary climbers' cafe serving massive portions.

---

## Day 2: Crib Goch's Knife Edge

### Morning
**Activity:** Crib Goch Guided Walk
Today, we're taking on Crib Goch, a legendary knife-edge arete that demands respect. This exposed scramble requires a head for heights and confident footwork. We'll tackle this with a qualified mountain guide, ensuring our safety and maximizing the experience.

**Travel:** 5 min drive to Pen-y-Pass

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Gorge Walking
Time to cool off and embrace the water! We'll plunge into a local gorge, scrambling up waterfalls, swimming in pools, and generally having a blast getting wet and wild. Another guided experience is highly recommended for this activity.

### Evening
**Stay:** Bunkhouse
Back to our trusty bunkhouse for a good night's sleep.

**Dinner suggestion:** The Heights, Llanberis - Pub grub with great views.

---

## Day 3: Underground Exploration and Descent

### Morning
**Activity:** Underground Adventure
Let's delve into the depths of Snowdonia with an Underground Adventure. Explore abandoned mines, navigate tunnels, and discover the hidden history beneath our feet. This is a unique perspective on the region's industrial past.

**Travel:** 20 min drive to the mines.

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Mountain Biking - MinorTaur
After the darkness of the mines, let's enjoy some fresh air on the trails with some mountain biking. We'll hit the MinorTaur trail, a great option for all skill levels and a fantastic way to wind down after the intensity of the previous days.

### Evening
**Departure:** Reflect on our incredible journey as we head home, filled with memories of conquering Snowdonia's summits.

## Tips

- Book your Crib Goch guide and Snowdon Summit Hike well in advance, especially during peak season.
- Pack for all weather conditions: waterproofs, layers, sturdy hiking boots, and a hat and gloves are essential.
- The Snowdonia National Park Authority website is a great resource for trail information, weather updates, and safety advice.
