## 2. Content Types Researched

### 2.1 Tandem Skydiving (First-Timers)

**What It Is:**
Strapped securely to experienced instructor who handles everything. Perfect for first-time jumpers.

**Who It's For:**
- Complete beginners (no experience needed)
- Bucket list tickers
- Gift experience recipients
- Anyone 16+

**The Experience:**
1. Arrive and check in (paperwork, medical form)
2. 30-45 min safety briefing and training
3. Get kitted up (jumpsuit, harness, helmet, goggles)
4. Board aircraft and scenic flight to altitude
5. **Exit plane** - the moment of truth!
6. **Freefall** - 15-60 seconds at 125mph
7. Parachute opens around 5,000ft
8. 4-6 minute scenic parachute ride
9. Land safely and celebrate!

**Duration:** Allow 3-4 hours total  
**Price:** ¬£250-¬£320 depending on location and height  
**Best Time:** March-November (summer peak season)

### 2.5 What to Expect on the Day

**Timeline:**
- **00:00** - Arrive at dropzone
- **00:15** - Check in, complete paperwork, pay balance
- **00:30** - Safety briefing begins (body position, exit procedure, landing)
- **01:15** - Get kitted up (meet your instructor)
- **01:30** - Board aircraft
- **01:45** - Reach altitude, prepare to jump
- **01:47** - **JUMP!**
- **01:48** - Freefall (15-60 seconds of pure adrenaline)
- **01:49** - Parachute opens (peaceful ride down)
- **01:54** - Land safely
- **02:00** - Debrief, collect certificate, celebrate!
- **02:30** - Head home with epic memories (and hopefully video!)

**What Happens:**
- **Freefall feels like:** Floating/flying rather than falling - the air resistance makes you feel suspended
- **Noise level:** VERY loud during freefall (wind rushing)
- **Temperature:** Cold at altitude - wear layers
- **Parachute opening:** Gentle tug, then sudden quiet and peace
- **Landing:** Simple - lift legs up, instructor guides you in

### 2.6 Age/Weight/Health Requirements

**Age:**
- Minimum: 16 years old
- Under 18: Parental consent + witnessed BPA medical form
- 16-39: Sign BPA medical form 115-C
- 40-69: Requires BPA medical form 115A
- 70+: Requires GP sign-off + pre-booking assessment at centre

**No maximum age** - but medical requirements increase with age.

**Weight:**
- **Typical:** 15-18 stone maximum (varies by centre)
- **Minimum:** 7 stone (44kg)
- **Haverfordwest (stricter):** 15 stone (men), 13 stone (women)
- **Swansea:** 18 stone max (surcharges: ¬£10/15-16st, ¬£20/16-17st, ¬£30/17-18st)
- **Important:** Must be proportionate to height

**Height:**
- Minimum: 4ft 11in (150cm)
- No maximum

**Health:**
- Must complete BPA medical declaration form
- GP approval needed for certain conditions:
  - Heart conditions
  - Epilepsy/fits
  - Diabetes (insulin-dependent)
  - Mental health medications
  - Recent surgery/injuries
  - Pregnancy (not permitted)
- Reasonable fitness required - must be able to:
  - Walk to aircraft
  - Hold body position during freefall
  - Lift legs for landing

**No alcohol/drugs:**
- No alcohol 24 hours before jump
- No recreational drugs
- Some prescription medications may be issue - declare on form

**What to Wear:**
- Comfortable clothes (jeans + t-shirt fine)
- Trainers or boots (no sandals, loose shoes)
- Warm layers (cold at altitude)
- Avoid:
  - Loose jewellery
  - Scarves
  - Anything that could catch in lines/equipment

### 2.7 Best Time of Year

**Operating Season:** March - November (8-9 months)

**Best Months:**

**üåû Summer (June-August):**
- ‚úÖ Best weather
- ‚úÖ Longest days
- ‚úÖ Warmest temperatures
- ‚ùå Books up fast
- ‚ùå Busiest period
- ‚ùå Higher chance of delays (busy skies)

**üçÉ Spring (April-May):**
- ‚úÖ Good weather improving
- ‚úÖ Quieter than summer
- ‚úÖ Clearer visibility (less haze)
- ‚úÖ Easter holiday opportunities
- ‚ùå Can still be cold

**üçÇ Autumn (September-October):**
- ‚úÖ Often excellent weather
- ‚úÖ Quieter bookings
- ‚úÖ Beautiful autumn colours visible
- ‚úÖ Still warm enough
- ‚ùå Season winding down
- ‚ùå Days getting shorter

**‚ùÑÔ∏è Winter (November-March):**
- ‚ùå Most centres closed or very limited operations
- ‚ùå Weather unreliable
- ‚ùå Short days
- ‚úÖ Occasional winter jump days (Swansea sometimes operates)

**Weather Dependency:**
- ALL skydiving is weather-dependent
- Rain = no jumping
- High winds = no jumping
- Low cloud = no jumping
- Poor visibility = no jumping
- **Be flexible** - you may need to reschedule
- Check with dropzone on your jump day if uncertain

**Booking Advice:**
- Book early for summer weekends
- Be flexible with dates if possible
- Consider weekdays (quieter, may get more personal attention)
- Spring/autumn often have better value and availability
