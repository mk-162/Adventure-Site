# Photography Adventure

This trip is designed for photography enthusiasts of all skill levels who want to explore and capture the breathtaking landscapes of Snowdonia. Expect a blend of iconic viewpoints, hidden gems, and opportunities to photograph Welsh culture. From sunrise over the mountains to the tranquil beauty of lakes, this itinerary offers a diverse range of photographic subjects.

## Day 1: Mountain Views and Lakeside Reflections

### Morning
**Activity:** Llanberis Path Guided Walk
The Llanberis Path offers stunning panoramic views of Snowdonia, perfect for capturing classic mountain landscapes. This gradual ascent is suitable for all fitness levels and provides plenty of opportunities to stop and photograph the scenery.

**Travel:** 15 min drive to Llyn Padarn

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Photography around Llyn Padarn
Capture the reflections of the mountains in the still waters of Llyn Padarn. This location offers great photographic opportunities with the ruined Dolbadarn Castle as a focal point. Consider a walk around the lake to find different vantage points.

### Evening
**Stay:** Hostel in Llanberis
A budget-friendly option that provides a comfortable base for exploring the area. Offers a great place to swap stories and tips with other travellers.

**Dinner suggestion:** Pete's Eats - a legendary mountaineering cafe known for its hearty portions.

---

## Day 2: Slate History and Waterfall Wonders

### Morning
**Activity:** Underground Adventure
Explore the fascinating world of a slate mine and capture the textures and colours within. This offers a unique photographic opportunity different from the expansive landscapes.

**Travel:** 20 min drive to Swallow Falls

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Photography at Swallow Falls
Swallow Falls is one of the most visited waterfalls in Wales, and rightfully so. Find the perfect angle to capture the power and beauty of the cascading water. Consider using a slow shutter speed for a dreamy effect.

### Evening
**Stay:** Bunkhouse near Betws-y-Coed
A rustic bunkhouse provides a comfortable and affordable base for exploring the surrounding woodlands. A great option for groups.

**Dinner suggestion:** Hangin' Pizzeria - Enjoy freshly prepared pizza in a cosy location.

---

## Day 3: Forest Fun and Scenic Routes

### Morning
**Activity:** Mountain Biking - MinorTaur
Explore Gwydir Forest by bike. This easy mountain bike trail will take you through beautiful woodlands, allowing you to capture the scenery whilst exercising.

**Travel:** 30 min drive to Pen-y-Pass

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Photography from Pen-y-Pass
Pen-y-Pass offers stunning views across the Snowdon Massif. It's the highest point accessible by car in Wales, making it an ideal spot for capturing panoramic photographs.

### Evening
**Stay:** Campsite/Bunkhouse near Llanberis.
Enjoy a relaxing stay at a comfortable campsite or bunkhouse after your adventures.

**Dinner suggestion:** The Heights - a pub with good views and hearty food.

## Tips

- Book your Llanberis Path Guided Walk in advance, especially during peak season.
- Pack a waterproof jacket and camera cover – the weather in Snowdonia can be unpredictable.
- Ask locals for hidden photography spots - they often know the best viewpoints!
