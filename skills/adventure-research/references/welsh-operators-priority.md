1. **Operator websites** (primary — most accurate for pricing/sessions)
2. **Google Maps** (rating, review count, address, coordinates)
3. **Adventure Smart Wales** (adventuresmart.uk.com — safety/qualification info)
4. **AALA** (aala.org.uk — check licence status)
5. **Visit Wales** (visitwales.com — official tourism listings)
6. **TripAdvisor** (URL only, for linking)
