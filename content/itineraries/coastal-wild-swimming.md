# Coastal Wild Swimming

This trip is for the adventurous soul who loves the thrill of cold water and breathtaking coastal scenery. Expect crystal clear waters, hidden coves, and a chance to reconnect with nature. The Pembrokeshire coast path will be your guide as you explore the best wild swimming spots in the region, combined with optional activities to suit your adventure level.

## Day 1: Coastal Exploration

### Morning
**Activity:** Wild Swimming
Explore a secluded cove along the Pembrokeshire Coast Path. We'll guide you to a safe and beautiful spot with crystal-clear water, perfect for an initial dip and acclimatisation. Consider a wetsuit if you're not accustomed to colder water!

**Travel:** 30 min drive to St Davids

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Boat Trip - Ramsey Island
Take a boat trip to Ramsey Island, a haven for wildlife. Keep an eye out for seals, dolphins, and a variety of seabirds. You can admire the coast from the boat.

### Evening
**Stay:** Eco Lodge
Relax in a comfortable eco lodge. Enjoy the peaceful surroundings and unwind after a day of adventures.

**Dinner suggestion:** The Bishops - Great food with local ingredients and sea views.

---

## Day 2: Adventure & Relaxation

### Morning
**Activity:** Coasteering Classic
Experience the Pembrokeshire coastline from a different perspective. Coasteering involves navigating the rocky shoreline, swimming in hidden gullies, and jumping from exhilarating heights (optional!).

**Travel:** 15 min drive/walk to a nearby beach

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Wild Swimming
Find another fantastic swim spot, this time focusing on a beach known for its calm waters. Enjoy the serenity of the sea and the breathtaking coastal views. Take a leisurely stroll afterwards on the beach.

### Evening
**Stay:** Campsite
Enjoy a night under canvas at a local campsite. Listen to the sound of the waves as you drift off to sleep.

**Dinner suggestion:** Cook your own food over the campfire, maybe a nice BBQ

---

## Day 3: Kayak & Calm

### Morning
**Activity:** Sea Kayaking Safari
Paddle along the coastline, exploring hidden coves and sea caves. Get up close and personal with the local marine life and enjoy a unique perspective of the Pembrokeshire coast.

**Travel:** 20 min drive to Stackpole Quay.

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Wild Swimming
Enjoy a final wild swim. After your sea kayaking, you will have plenty of opportunities to find your own secluded spot and have one last dip in the Atlantic.

### Evening
**Stay:** Hostel
Relax at the hostel and maybe play some board games with other travellers.

**Dinner suggestion:** A local pub - Enjoy traditional pub fare.
