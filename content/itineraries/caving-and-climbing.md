# Caving & Climbing

This trip is for adrenaline junkies who aren't afraid to get muddy and push their limits. You'll explore the hidden world beneath the Brecon Beacons with a challenging caving expedition and then test your strength and skill on the crags above. Expect stunning views, exhilarating challenges, and a serious sense of accomplishment. This itinerary is physically demanding; a good level of fitness is essential.

## Day 1: Descent into Darkness

### Morning
**Activity:** Caving
Delve into the heart of the Brecon Beacons with a guided caving adventure. You'll navigate tight passages, scramble over rocks, and marvel at the underground formations. Be prepared to get wet and muddy!

**Travel:** 15 min drive to caving location.

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Caving
Continue the caving adventure, exploring deeper into the cave system and tackling more challenging obstacles. This afternoon is designed to test your teamwork and problem-solving skills in a unique environment.

### Evening
**Stay:** Bunkhouse
A comfortable and budget-friendly option to rest and recover after a day of caving. Perfect for swapping stories with fellow adventurers.

**Dinner suggestion:** The Old Ford Inn - A traditional pub serving hearty meals and local ales.

---

## Day 2: Ascend to Glory

### Morning
**Activity:** Rock Climbing
Rise and shine for a day of rock climbing! A qualified instructor will guide you through the basics of climbing techniques and safety procedures. You'll tackle a variety of routes to suit your skill level, from beginner-friendly slabs to challenging overhangs.

**Travel:** 20 min drive to climbing crag.

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Rock Climbing
Continue climbing, pushing your limits and enjoying the stunning views from the top of the crag. Focus on developing your technique and building confidence on the rock.

### Evening
**Stay:** Campsite
Enjoy a night under the stars at a scenic campsite. A great opportunity to reflect on your adventure and enjoy the peace and quiet of the Brecon Beacons.

**Dinner suggestion:** Cook your own meal on a camping stove - Pack some sausages or burgers to celebrate your achievements.

## Tips

- Book your caving and climbing activities in advance, especially during peak season.
- Pack waterproof clothing, sturdy footwear, and a headlamp for caving.
- Check the weather forecast before you go and be prepared for all conditions. The Brecon Beacons weather can change rapidly.
