# Kayaking Explorer

Ready to ditch the crowds and explore Anglesey from a completely different perspective? This 3-day kayaking adventure is designed for those with some paddling experience, or a good level of fitness and a willingness to learn. Expect exhilarating sea kayaking, secluded beaches only accessible by water, and the chance to spot seals, seabirds, and maybe even a porpoise or two. Get ready for a proper dose of coastal exploration!

## Day 1: Coastal Discovery

### Morning
**Activity:** Sea Kayaking
Explore the coastline near Holyhead. You'll hug the cliffs, venture into sea caves (tide permitting!), and get a feel for the sea. Keep an eye out for guillemots and razorbills nesting on the cliffs.

**Travel:** 20 min drive to Trearddur Bay.

### Afternoon
**Activity:** RIB Boat Ride
After a morning of paddling, swap paddles for horsepower with a thrilling RIB boat ride. See the Skerries lighthouse and experience the exhilaration of speeding along the coastline.

### Evening
**Stay:** B&B in Trearddur Bay.
Enjoy a comfortable stay with stunning sea views, perfect for unwinding after a day on the water.

**Dinner suggestion:** The Sea Shanty Cafe - Fresh seafood with a relaxed atmosphere.

---

## Day 2: Island Circumnavigation (Partial)

### Morning
**Activity:** Sea Kayaking
Today's the big one: a longer paddle exploring the Menai Strait. We'll launch near Beaumaris and head towards Bangor, taking in the impressive bridges and historic castles from the water.

**Travel:** 30 min drive to Beaumaris.

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Coasteering
After a packed lunch on a remote beach, its time for some fun! Head to a Coasteering spot nearby and spend the afternoon clambering, jumping, and swimming along the coast. Expect adrenaline and a few splashes.

### Evening
**Stay:** Self-catering cottage near Beaumaris.
Relax in a cozy self-catering cottage. Perfect for drying gear and cooking up a hearty meal.

**Dinner suggestion:** The Gazelle Hotel - Gastro pub with a great menu and local ales.

---

## Day 3: Wind and Waves

### Morning
**Activity:** Kitesurfing
Time to try something completely different! Head to Rhosneigr, a popular kitesurfing beach, for an introductory lesson. Learn the basics of kite control and feel the power of the wind.

**Travel:** 45 min drive to Rhosneigr.

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Free time in Rhosneigr.
Spend some time browsing the local surf shops, relaxing on the beach, or grabbing lunch with a view.

### Evening
**Stay:** Campsite/Caravans near Rhosneigr.
A budget-friendly option to end your adventure. Enjoy the fresh air and reflect on your kayaking exploits.

**Dinner suggestion:** Catch 22 Brasserie - A great place for fish and chips.

## Tips

- Book your kayaking and coasteering activities in advance, especially during peak season, to avoid disappointment.
- Pack layers of clothing, including waterproofs, as the weather in Anglesey can change quickly.
- Check the tide times before planning your kayaking routes. The Menai Strait can have strong currents.
