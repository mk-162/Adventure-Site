Quick Answer: Difficulty Level

Most of our itineraries are rated 'Moderate'. This means you should be comfortable walking 4-6 hours a day on uneven terrain. No technical climbing skills are required.

What happens if the weather is bad? expand_more
Welsh weather can be unpredictable! We hike rain or shine, but safety is paramount. In extreme conditions (high winds, lightning), our guides may alter the route or offer alternative low-level activities.
Is equipment provided? expand_more
We provide technical gear like helmets and crampons if needed for specific winter routes. You are responsible for your personal clothing, hiking boots, and a daypack. A full kit list is sent upon booking.
Are meals vegetarian friendly? expand_more
Absolutely. We cater to all dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free. Please let us know your preferences when completing your booking form.
