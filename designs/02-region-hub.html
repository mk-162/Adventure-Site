Welcome to Snowdonia

Eryri (Snowdonia) is the adventure capital of Wales. From the highest peak in Wales and England to the stunning coastline, it's a diverse landscape begging to be explored. Whether you're looking for heart-pounding zip lines, tranquil hikes, or historic castles, you'll find it here. The region is home to nine mountain ranges, covering 52% of the park, and includes 74 miles of coastline.

Getting There
expand_more
Trains run directly to Bangor and Llandudno Junction. From there, the Conwy Valley Line takes you into the heart of the park. By car, the A55 provides easy access from Chester and Liverpool.

Best Time to Visit
expand_more
May to September offers the best weather, though shoulder seasons (April/October) are less crowded and still beautiful. Winter brings snow to the peaks for experienced mountaineers.

Essential Gear
expand_more
Waterproof jacket and layers are essential year-round. For hiking, bring sturdy boots, a map, and extra food/water. Weather changes rapidly in the mountains.
