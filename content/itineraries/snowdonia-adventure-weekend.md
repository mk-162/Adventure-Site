# Snowdonia Adventure Weekend

This action-packed Snowdonia weekend is designed for those who love to be active and explore the outdoors. Get ready to challenge yourself with a Snowdon summit hike, experience the thrill of Velocity 2, and delve into the depths of a Welsh mine. This itinerary balances iconic Snowdonia experiences with a taste of the region's diverse activities.

## Day 1: Summit Challenge

### Morning
**Activity:** Snowdon Summit Hike
Conquer the highest peak in Wales! We'll tackle one of the classic routes (Llanberis Path if you want something more gradual, Miners' Track for a more scenic experience). Get ready for breathtaking views and a real sense of accomplishment.

**Travel:** 10 min drive to Llanberis/Pen-y-Pass (depending on chosen route).

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Underground Adventure
Descend into a former slate mine for a unique exploration experience. Discover the history and geology of the mine on a guided tour. This will provide a great contrast to the open mountain of the morning.

### Evening
**Stay:** Hostel in Llanberis
Basic but comfortable accommodation, perfect for budget-conscious adventurers. It's a great place to meet other outdoor enthusiasts.

**Dinner suggestion:** Pete's Eats - legendary climbers' cafe serving hearty portions.

---

## Day 2: Adrenaline Rush

### Morning
**Activity:** Velocity 2 Zip Line
Soar through the skies on the fastest zip line in the world! Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush as you fly over the stunning Penrhyn Quarry. This is an unforgettable experience.

**Travel:** 30 min drive to Penrhyn Quarry.

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Zip World Fforest Coaster
After the high speed of the zip line, try a more sedate but equally fun toboggan run through the forest. Enjoy the twists and turns as you descend through the trees.

### Evening
**Stay:** Bunkhouse near Betws-y-Coed
Rustic bunkhouse offering a comfortable and affordable base for exploring the area. Perfect for groups.

**Dinner suggestion:** Olif gastro pub in Betws-y-Coed - serving locally sourced Welsh food.

---

## Day 3: Valley Views & Gorge Scramble

### Morning
**Activity:** Mountain Biking - MinorTaur
Head to the Gwydir Forest for a thrilling mountain biking adventure. The MinorTaur trail offers a fantastic blend of flowy sections and technical challenges. Bring your own bike or hire locally.

**Travel:** 5 min drive to Gwydir Forest.

### Afternoon
**Activity:** Gorge Walking
Get wet and wild as you scramble up a stunning gorge! Navigate waterfalls, plunge pools, and rock formations under the guidance of experienced instructors. Prepare to be challenged and have a blast.

### Evening
**Stay:** Campsite/Bunkhouse in Capel Curig
Enjoy the last night under the stars or in a bunkhouse, reflecting on the amazing adventures of the weekend.

**Dinner suggestion:** Moel Siabod Cafe - Offers stunning views and good food.

## Tips

- Book activities and accommodation in advance, especially during peak season.
- Pack for all types of weather – Snowdonia can be unpredictable! Layers are key.
- Check the weather forecast before setting off on any mountain activities. Safety first!
