### Ranking Criteria by Activity

**Beaches:**
1. Beauty/scenery
2. Water quality
3. Facilities
4. Accessibility
5. Crowd levels
6. Activities available

**Surf Breaks:**
1. Wave quality/consistency
2. Range of conditions
3. Facilities
4. Crowd factor
5. Beginner friendliness
6. Water quality

**MTB Trails:**
1. Trail quality
2. Variety/range
3. Facilities
4. Scenery
5. Value for money
6. Progression options

**Hiking:**
1. Scenery/views
2. Route quality
3. Challenge/reward ratio
4. Accessibility
5. Crowds
6. Navigation ease

**Wild Swimming:**
1. Water quality
2. Setting/beauty
3. Accessibility
4. Safety
5. Facilities nearby
6. Temperature

## 12. Content Production Workflow

### For each "Best Of" page:

1. **Query database** for spots matching criteria
2. **Rank spots** using defined criteria + editorial judgment
3. **Write unique intro** (150+ words, not template)
4. **Write spot entries** (100+ words each, specific to why it ranks)
5. **Add data cards** from database
6. **Add photos** (1+ per spot)
7. **Add internal links** (profiles, operators, related)
8. **Add schema markup** (ItemList, Place)
9. **Quality check** against checklist
10. **Publish** and add to sitemap

### Estimated time per page:
- "Best of" list: 2-3 hours
- Location page: 1-2 hours  
- Spot profile: 30-60 minutes (data-driven)
