3-Day Snowdonia Adventure Weekend

Conquer the highest peak in Wales, explore ancient slate caverns, and experience the thrill of the world's fastest zip line.

Daily Itinerary

flag

Arrival & Briefing

Day 1 - 16:00

Meet your guides and fellow adventurers at our base camp in Llanberis. We'll go over the kit list, weather forecast, and safety briefing before enjoying a welcome dinner at a local Welsh pub.

Overnight Rest
hiking

Summit Hike

Day 2 - 09:00

We take the Pyg Track up to the summit of Snowdon (Yr Wyddfa). This moderate route offers stunning views of the lakes below. Lunch on the summit before descending via the Miners Track.
